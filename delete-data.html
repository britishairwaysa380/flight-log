Delete Your Data

You can delete your FlightDeck account and all associated data at any time, directly from the app — no email required.

How to delete your account
1
Open the FlightDeck app and make sure you're signed in.
2
Tap your Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
3
Tap the Settings icon (⚙️) in the top right corner.
4
Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
5
Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account and all data will be permanently removed.
⚠️ This action is permanent. Once deleted, your account, flights, photos, and all personal data cannot be recovered.
What gets deleted
Your account and profile information
All flight logs and records
Photos and captions you've uploaded
Comments, likes, and social activity
Followers and following relationships
Notifications and account history
