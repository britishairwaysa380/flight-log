How to use FlightDeck
Your personal aviation logbook
✈ Step 1 — Tap the + Button

Hit the + button (bottom right on mobile, or the Log a Flight button on desktop). Enter your flight number (e.g. UA1234) and date, then tap Look Up Flight. FlightDeck auto-fills the route, airline, duration, and distance.

✏️ Step 2 — Add Optional Details

After the lookup, you can add aircraft type (e.g. Boeing 737-824), tail number (registration), and personal notes. Find these on FlightRadar24.com — search your flight and scroll down for the aircraft details.

🗺️ Map & Filters

Tap any airport dot on the map to filter all stats and charts to that airport. Tap the Clear button in the tab bar to reset. Use the date chips (30d, 90d, 1y) or pick a custom date range in the Flights tab.

👥 Social Features

The Feed shows flights from people you follow. Use Discover to find other flyers. Follow requests are private — you control who sees your flights. Set visibility per-flight: Public, Followers Only, or Private.

📱 Install as an App

iPhone: Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen.
Android: Chrome → three dots → Add to Home Screen.
FlightDeck opens full-screen like a native app with offline support.

💡 Tip: Only your flight number and date are required. Everything else is optional — the more you fill in, the richer your Dashboard and Trends become.
